Departments

Face Brick

We are pleased to provide many manufacturers of beautiful clay brick. We have a showroom of samples or our salesman is happy to bring samples directly to you.

Paving Brick

Unilock paving brick is inspired by the timeless beauty of European brick pavers. We offer the complete line and stock the most popular styles.

Landscape Stone

Build your customers patio with the finest Pennsylvania bluestone and Eden Stone limestone. These are the most popular on the market for a reason.


Fireplaces

We carry a full selection of fireplace products. We have firebrick, chimney pots, dampers, Rumford components and much more always in stock.

Cement

We supply portland cement, type N and S masonry cement, and preblended mortar. We have colored pre-blends to suit all of your needs.

Steel

Don’t forget Rebar and primed Angle Iron for your site. Additionally, we have ladder and truss wire for block wall construction.


Limestone Sills

We have the finest quality Limestone sills with standard sizes from 2.25”x5” to 4”x10” in stock. Coping and keystones are also available.

Aggregates

Mason sand, crushed limestone, and gravel are available for pickup or delivery. Whether for mortar mixes or landscape bases, we have the aggregate.

Block

We stock standard block in sizes from 4” to 12” as well as bond-beam block. We source specialty sizes and textures to satisfy your project.
